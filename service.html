Imperial Driving School Melbourne is your trusted partner for learning to drive and passing your driving test. We offer comprehensive driving lessons, test preparation, and all services related to your driving requirements. Our instructors are trained to deliver lessons according to VicRoads criteria, ensuring you become a professional and safe driver.

We proudly serve almost every suburb in the Melbourne metropolitan area and are continuously expanding to meet the growing demand of our valued customers. Our team includes former VicRoads testing officers, making us unique in the industry and giving you the best chance of success.

We guide our students step by step, ensuring a stress-free process from start to finish. We also offer online classes for the learner permit knowledge test and hazard perception test, so you feel confident when attempting your tests.

Our instructors are authorized to accompany you during your driving test, helping you feel relaxed and confident. If you need a car for your test, we offer affordable car hire for test purposes.

If you are an overseas license holder planning to convert your license to a Victorian one, just give us a call. We have helped thousands of overseas drivers pass their test on the first attempt.

We provide detailed training before your test, so you know exactly what to do and what to avoid. Our approach is always tailored to your individual needs, ensuring the best possible outcome.

We have been serving driving education with more branches and completed 2k+ students in last 4 years. We have a team of experienced and certified trainers who will help you from the start to end of a driving lesson.
