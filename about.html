Imperial Driving School's ultimate aim is to provide quality driving lessons and enable students to pass their driving test with confidence. Our instructors equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to confidently obtain your Victorian Drivers Licence.

By doing so, we contribute responsible and safe drivers to the community, giving you lifelong skills for anywhere in Victoria.

Why Choose Imperial Driving School?

  • Ex VicRoads Testing Officers

    Our instructors are former VicRoads testing officers, making us unique among competitors. We provide training that strictly follows VicRoads criteria, ensuring you pass your test on the first attempt and save time and money.

  • Easy to Learn

    We make learning to drive comfortable and stress-free, helping you build confidence during lessons and your driving test.

  • Career Opportunities

    Many jobs require a valid driver's license. Our lessons help you open new career opportunities by ensuring you pass your test on the first attempt.

  • Safe Road Users

    We are dedicated to providing the highest quality driving education, helping you become a safe, confident, and skilled driver.

  • Teaching All Ages

    Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, our expert instructors can help you achieve your driving goals.

  • Better Social Life

    Driving gives you the freedom to attend social events and make new connections, thanks to the confidence you gain from our lessons.

  • Family Responsibilities

    Our training helps you fulfill family responsibilities, like school drop-offs or caring for loved ones. Driving lessons and passing your driving test make it possible.
